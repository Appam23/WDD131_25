Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase and celebrate the rich cultural diversity and vibrant traditions of Northeast India. It provides visitors with authentic information on unique customs, festivals, art, cuisine, and heritage of its many indigenous communities. The site aims to foster appreciation and understanding among a global audience while serving as a valuable resource for travelers, students, and researchers.

Audience

The audience for this website includes travel enthusiasts and tourists interested in exploring Northeast India, students and researchers seeking information on the region’s diverse heritage, educators looking for reliable content on Indian tribal cultures, cultural enthusiasts and historians, members of Northeast Indian communities both local and abroad, the general public curious about India’s cultural diversity, and travel bloggers or content creators searching for authentic material and inspiration
